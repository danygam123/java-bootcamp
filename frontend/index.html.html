Java AP CSA Teaching
Hi! I'm a high school student who took AP Computer Science A and wanted to make the prep process easier for everyone who comes after me. Java can feel overwhelming at first, so I built this bootcamp to break it down into 6 clear, manageable sessions.
This bootcamp covers every major topic you'll see on the AP CSA exam — from Java basics and loops all the way to classes, objects, and ArrayLists. Everything is free, and you can go at your own pace or join live Saturday morning sessions.
